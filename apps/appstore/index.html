Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a free, multilingual online encyclopedia that allows users to contribute and edit its content. It was founded in 2001 by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger, and has since become one of the most widely used sources of information on the internet. Wikipedia contains over 6 million articles in English alone, covering a wide range of topics, from history and science to pop culture and current events. Its open editing system allows anyone with internet access to edit its articles, which has led to criticism and controversy over the accuracy and reliability of its content. Nonetheless, Wikipedia remains a valuable resource for information and research for millions of people around the world.
